DESIGN

We needed a way to package on-brand print and digital collateral in a customizable way. It had to elegantly give an overview of GalaxySuite products while differentiating them, so I created a "playing card" version of each product. With fun yet descriptive icons, we aimed for a design that felt more genuine and authentic while utilizing white space and vivid colors in a unique way.

PRODUCTS

The GalaxySuite is a collection of marketing services recently created by All Star's business development team. The sales team needed a way to selectively introduce certain services to customers. The collateral package would inform customers of the benefits to their business if they had this particular "card up their sleeve."

PROCESS

  • Finalized core messaging with copywriter, sales, and marketing
  • Provided options for product icons
  • Ideated on ways to deliver product information selectively to customers
  • Mocked up the idea of displaying information like playing cards
  • Presented mockups to teams for approval and feedback
  • Final tweaks before sending to print

All Star

Website

Home Makeover, Website Edition.

  • Wireframing
  • Web design & iconography
  • Front-end development

DESIGN

All Star had a new logo, but the website still reflected their old branding. We needed to update the design to align to our new direction with strong messaging on the following three attributes: Quality, Compliance, and Consistency. I worked with the creative director to make an experience that felt more genuine and authentic while utilizing white space and vivid colors in a unique way.

THE EVOLUTION

The creative director and I collaborated with content and business development to work out a style that would represent the company's changes and build an identity. All Star had undergone many changes to position itself as an industry leader, and they wanted this image to be loud and clear throughout the site's design and content. The rebrand was an effort to revitalize All Star's B-to-B image with customers and partners without alienating them with excessive change. Success of this endeavor would then influence rebranding for revenue-generating properties on the B-to-C side.

PROCESS

  • Work with business development, content team, and creative director to filter core copy
  • Collaborate with creative director to ideate designs and style
  • Select and finalize a design style
  • Build out mockups of each page
  • Coordinate photo shoots of executives, employees, and office space for assets
  • Build pages in HTML/CSS/Javascript
  • Debug and cross-browser test
