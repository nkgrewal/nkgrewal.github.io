UI & UX Style

Simple. Purposeful. Friendly. PRO is targeted to paying business users with clear intent. We're more "customer service" oriented than the WP consumer site.

Our ultimate goal is to make as easy as possible for users to quickly and efficiently complete their tasks.

Overall Style:
  • Clean, open layouts
  • Plenty of padding and minimal adornments to compliment our brand color
  • Content-first approach
  • Concise copy with an active voice
  • Minimizing to neccessities for each interaction
  • Thoughtfully using tools like icons and colors to give users visual queues

Visuals

Minimalistic, iconographic visuals help shape white space.
Using pure colors from our palette, keep shapes clean and flat by avoiding overused effects like drop shadows and gradients. Illustrations should reinforce brand personality. They should strike a balance of being friendly, straightforward, and smart without being cutesy or sterile.

Typography

  • Font family: Roboto
  • Default size: 15px
  • Base color: #555
  • Font weight: 300 (Roboto Light)

Stick to "Light" weight and above. With the lighter weight, typography steers towards large font sizes with a ample line-height. "Thin" weights tend to reproduce poorly on low resolution screens.

With a regular weight, the impact is heavier. In this case, shrink all text about 1-2px and use subtle font size differences to establish heirarchy. This weight works great for sparse text. Longer copy works better with a lighter weight.

Dropdowns

Stylability for dropdowns are limited unless you use Javascript. If you're styling in CSS, this gets it somewhat in line with our visual style

Dropdowns are useful, but tend to be unwieldy creatures. If you have a field with over 10 selections, it may be a chore for users. Stick to short lists and use another field if the the list is lengthy.
