UI & UX Style

Simple. Purposeful. Friendly.
Pro is targeted to paying business users. We're more "customer service" oriented than Whitepages.com. Our ultimate goal is to make as easy as possible for users to quickly and efficiently complete their tasks.

  • Clean, open layouts
  • Plenty of padding and minimal adornments
  • Content-first approach
  • Concise calls to action with an active voice
  • Minimizing to neccessities for each interaction
  • Sparingly use icons and colors to give users visual queues

Limit the use of other colors. Be careful when using the green or orange together as they are typically associated with warning and risk for business uses. The plum is a "just in case" color, but shouldn't be used where the other colors would suffice.

Typography

Body font

Stick to "Light" weight and above. "Thin" weights tend to reproduce poorly on low resolution screens. Roboto Regular (400) works better for sparse text.

Text links

Default links are #3c8fc9 some stuff and going to a place. Emphasis can be placed on strong elements.

Avoid using a multitude of text links, because it works against the minimal style. If you pick an accent color, use it consistently or it will throw too much color all over the place.

Minimalistic icons help shape white space.
Using pure colors from our palette, keep shapes clean and flat by avoiding overused effects like drop shadows and gradients. Illustrations should reinforce brand personality. They should strike a balance of being friendly, straightforward, and smart without being cutesy or sterile.

Buttons

Square, flat, with plenty of padding. This leads to strong calls to action The beveled and rounded ones work great for our form fields

Dropdowns

Stylability for dropdowns are limited unless you use Javascript. If you're styling in CSS, this gets it somewhat in line with our visual style

Dropdowns are useful, but tend to be unwieldy creatures. If you have a field with over 10 selections, it may be a chore for users. Stick to short lists and use another field if the the list is lengthy.
