SALARY RANGE*

$37,185 to $55,706

*from Salary.com

Social work careers are for the brave and visionary, for people who can—and do—change the world one life at a time. 2010 data from Salary.com shows that social worker salaries range from $37,185 to $55,706.

The average social worker salary is $46,363. Not too shabby when you factor in the reality that you are getting paid to dramatically improve the lives of people who may have nowhere else to turn.
