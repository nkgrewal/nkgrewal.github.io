A GROWING FIELD THAT OFFERS FINANCIAL OPPORTUNITIES

While your employer will determine your pay based on individual factors, it can be helpful to know that the middle 50 percent of dental assisting salaries fall between $29,677 and $36,240*. The top ten percent of earners in the dental assisting field make $39,055 or more.*

A dental assisting career can come with many advantages that go beyond salary. For instance, many dental assistant positions are salaried, which may allow you a regular paycheck, but the hours may be more appealing than in other professions. Private dental practices often have fewer hours (three or four days per week), allowing you an easy schedule that complements your lifestyle.

* Salary statistics from Salary.com
