A GROWING FIELD THAT OFFERS FINANCIAL OPPORTUNITIES

According to 2010 Salary.com data, the middle 50 percent of medical assistants earn between $27,451 and $32,714*. The top ten percent of earners in the medical assistant field make $35,195 or more. And there are great ways to increase your income and make you more valuable in your career.

A medical assistant degree is also a great stepping stone to other medical careers. Experienced medical assistants may be promoted to office manager or clinical supervisor, or you can parlay your expertise to become a medical assistant teacher.

* Salary statistics from Salary.com
