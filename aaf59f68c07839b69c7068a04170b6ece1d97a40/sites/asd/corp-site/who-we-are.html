Since 2001, All Star has been committed to one goal:

Helping students find the right schools and programs to help them fulfill their dreams.

Technology

Our agile technology team has created a best-of-breed, scalable platform that effectively and efficiently delivers information to prospective students and quality leads to our customers at a great value.

Business Operations

All Star's business operations team includes the human resources and finance groups, and also builds and maintains school fact sheets, program information, and student requests for information (RFIs), as well as transmits lead data to All Star's clients.

Business Intelligence

All Star's business intelligence group aggregates and analyzes extensive data sets to help match the most qualified students with the schools where they're most likely to succeed.

Marketing

All Star's marketing team encompasses the channel, merchandising and product marketing groups. Together they create and optimize SEM- and content-focused campaigns that resonate with aspiring students and encourage them to connect with the right schools.

Sales

Our account managers are on the front lines of managing and resolving any concerns that our customers might have at any time. They closely oversee our customers' inventory, budget and individual branding needs, helping to grow their business while ensuring there's no exposure to regulatory issues.
