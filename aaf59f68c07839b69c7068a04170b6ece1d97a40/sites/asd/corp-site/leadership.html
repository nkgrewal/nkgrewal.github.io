Doug Brown

President and Chief Executive Officer

Doug has more than 25 years of experience leading high-growth technology companies and joined All Star in 2005. Doug's been a member of more than 15 growth-stage and nonprofit boards of directors and co-founded the Education Marketing Council to establish and promote best marketing practices within post-secondary education. Doug was named a Regional Entrepreneur of the Year by Ernst & Young in 2011, and is a frequent speaker and mentor to aspiring and early-stage entrepreneurs. He graduated with honors from the University of Idaho with a bachelor's degree in business.

Bill Hummel

Director of Sales

Bill has worked in various roles since joining the company in 2007, including strategic planning and product and channel management. Prior to All Star, Bill worked for Amazon for more than seven years for product lines including books and corporate accounts. He has a Ph.D. in American literature from Arizona State University, from which he graduated with distinction.

Marston Gould

Vice President of Marketing

Marston brings more than 10 years of executive and strategic consulting experience. He is responsible for creating and leading marketing strategies for All Star. Marston has worked at NASA, Alaska Airlines, and eMaven. He received his MBA from the Darden Graduate School of Business at the University of Virginia.

Mark Cassidy

Vice President of Technology

Mark brought his more than 28 years of technology and executive management experience to All Star in 2010, having most recently served as VP of Engineering at Disney Interactive Media Group. He holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University.
